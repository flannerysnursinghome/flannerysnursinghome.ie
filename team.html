Our team

The people who make Flannery's home

Our people

Kind, experienced, and genuinely caring

Every member of our team — from nurses to healthcare assistants, from catering to reception — shares the same values: kindness, patience, and a deep commitment to the people in our care.

AV

Anoop Veliyath Asokan

Person in Charge (PIC)

Anoop leads the day-to-day operations of Flannery's, overseeing clinical governance, regulatory compliance, and the overall standard of care. He works closely with the clinical and management teams to ensure Flannery's continues to meet and exceed HIQA standards.

KF

Kathleen Flannery

Proprietor & Director

Kathleen founded Flannery's Nursing Home on the belief that every person deserves to feel genuinely at home in their care setting. Her family values remain the cornerstone of everything the home stands for today.

DT

Divya Thomas

Clinical Nurse Manager (CNM)

Divya leads our nursing team with expertise and compassion. She oversees clinical care planning, staff supervision, and ensures that every resident receives care that meets the highest professional standards.

FF

Fiona Finnerty

Receptionist & Health & Safety Representative

Fiona is often the first point of contact for families and visitors. She manages front-of-house operations and serves as our Health & Safety Representative.

Our wider team

Beyond our leadership team, Flannery's is supported by a dedicated team of over 58 staff across nursing, healthcare assistance, catering, maintenance, activities, and administration. Every person plays a vital role in creating the warm, caring environment our residents call home.
