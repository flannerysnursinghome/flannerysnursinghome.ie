Admissions

Starting your journey with Flannery's

How it works

A simple, supported admission process

We understand that choosing a nursing home is one of the most significant decisions a family makes. Our admissions team is here to guide you through every step, with no pressure and no jargon.

1

Get in touch

Call us on 093 43130 or fill in the enquiry form. We'll respond within one working day to discuss your situation and answer any questions.

2

Visit us

We warmly invite you to come and see Flannery's for yourself. Meet the team, see the rooms, and get a feel for life here. No obligation whatsoever.

3

Assessment

A pre-admission assessment is completed to ensure we can meet the individual's care needs. We guide you through it clearly and simply.

4

Paperwork & contracts

We complete all necessary contracts and documentation with you, clearly explaining all charges and arrangements. No surprises.

5

Moving in

We work with you to make moving day as smooth and comfortable as possible. Residents are encouraged to bring personal belongings to make their room feel like home from day one.

Make an enquiry

We respond within one working day (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm).

Your information is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and GDPR obligations and will not be shared with third parties.

What to bring

Moving in — what to expect

We encourage residents to personalise their rooms with familiar items from home.

Clothing & personal items

Comfortable clothing for all seasons, footwear, nightwear, toiletries, and personal grooming items. All clothing should be labelled with the resident's name.

Personal effects

Photos, books, a favourite cushion, ornaments — anything that makes a room feel like home. We actively encourage residents to personalise their space.

Medical information

Medical card, GP details, list of current medications, and any relevant medical history. Our team will guide you on exactly what is needed.
